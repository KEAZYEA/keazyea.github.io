Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Keazyea's Intelligence Hub ("we", "our", "the site") collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. This site is an unofficial fan-made companion hub and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the publisher of the game it covers.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Google Sign-In data: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. Signing in is optional — most features work without it.
  • Profile information: If you're signed in, we store your chosen in-game display name and avatar selection in our database (Firebase/Firestore) so it can sync across your devices.
  • Giveaway entries: If you enter the weekly VIP giveaway, we store your name, email, and selected device type (iOS/Android) to run the draw and deliver prizes.
  • Subscription status: If you subscribe to VIP or No Ads, we store your subscription status and expiration date. Payment itself is handled entirely by PayPal — we never see or store your card, bank, or PayPal login details.
  • Inbox messages: Notifications (like promo code alerts or giveaway results) are stored locally in your browser and are not tied to your account across devices.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdSense to show ads to non-subscribers. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites.

How We Use Information

  • To let you sign in and keep your profile/progress synced across devices
  • To run the weekly giveaway and contact winners about their prize
  • To manage VIP/No Ads subscription access
  • To show relevant ads to visitors who haven't subscribed to remove them
  • To respond to support questions you send us

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third parties, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and database hosting)
  • PayPal (subscription billing — see PayPal's Privacy Statement)
  • Google AdSense (advertising — see how Google uses information from sites that use its services). You can manage your ad personalization settings at Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for sign-in sessions and, for non-subscribers, ad personalization through Google AdSense. Subscribing to the No Ads or VIP plan removes ads (and the associated ad cookies) site-wide.

Data Retention & Your Choices

You can sign out at any time, which stops your profile from syncing further. To request that we delete your account data (profile, giveaway entries, prize history), email us at the address below and we'll process the request. Locally stored inbox messages can be cleared by clearing your browser's site data.

Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
